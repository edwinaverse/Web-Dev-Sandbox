The Best Movies According to Edwina

My top 3 movies I've watched this year.

Grave of The Fireflies (1988)

Anime movie that always brings me to tears. Will always recommend.

Waves (2019)

A fantastic discussion on the overlooked nature of daughters in African American households.

Joker (2019)

An unsettling, thrilling experience as you follow the Joker's progressive build of insanity throughout the course of the movie.
